Truffles


Truffles can be known as (Tuber melanosporum, Tuber magnatum, etc) are an edible form of mushroom , not to be confused with the chocolate treat. Truffles are actually quite valuable.Pound for pound, truffle is one of the most expensive foods you can buy. The reason behind such high costs is the scarcity of the produce, truffles are seasonal , extremely difficult to grow and take many years to cultivate. They also have a short shelf life.
