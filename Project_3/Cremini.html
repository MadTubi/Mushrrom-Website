Cremini


This is a Cremini mushroom, also known as Agaricus bisporus.Cremini, also known as crimini mushrooms, are simply a different strain than the white button. Developed by commercial growers, they are brown in color and have a slightly deeper taste.

How to cook Cremini

A note on eating cremini, do be aware that Agaricus bisporus contains small amounts of toxins (gyromitrin for example). These toxins are rendered to negligible levels by heat so always cook your mushrooms. Many sources will tell you it’s safe to eat them raw, but the truth is it’s best to cook your mushrooms.
