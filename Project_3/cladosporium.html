Cladosporium


This is a type of mold called cladosporium. Cladosporium mold is extremely common both indoors and outdoors, consisting of more than 700 different varieties. It is most often found in decaying organic material and soil, on food, and textiles. It can easily spread within the home, especially when the spores come into contact with dark, damp surfaces. Poor ventilation and high humidity levels also contribute to the growth of this type of mold.
