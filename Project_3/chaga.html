Chaga


This mushroom is called Chaga ,also known as inonotus obliquus, is a parasitic fungus that grows on birch trees. It has a long history as a medicinal mushroom in Russian and Polish folk medicine.

The health benefits of chaga is that its strengthens the immune system, has powerful anti-oxidant properties, gastro-intestinal support, support for cancer patients, and strong anti-bacterial and anti-viral properties.
